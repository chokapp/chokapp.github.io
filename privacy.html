Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Chok is built to keep your skincare data yours. We designed it to collect as little as possible.

What we store, and where

Your routines, logs, photos, and skin profile are stored on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, this data syncs privately between your own devices through Apple's iCloud (your private CloudKit database). We cannot read it. There is no Chok account and no Chok server that stores your personal data.

No tracking

Chok contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. We do not build a profile of you and we do not sell or share data.

AI features (Chok Pro)

If you subscribe to Chok Pro and use an AI feature (Coach chat, Photo-to-Shelf matching, routine drafting, weekly insights), the text needed for that request is sent to our processing service to generate a response. Photo-to-Shelf processes images on your device using Apple's Vision framework — photo images never leave your device; only the recognized text is sent for product matching. AI requests are not linked to an identity (there is no account) and are not used to advertise to you.

HealthKit

If you enable Skin Cycling's optional cycle awareness, Chok reads your menstrual cycle dates from HealthKit to predict luteal-phase breakouts. This data is used only on your device and is never transmitted. Denying access does not block the app.

Children

Chok is not directed to children under 13.

Your control

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings inside the app, or by deleting the app and removing its iCloud data from your Apple ID settings.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email chokapp@proton.me.
